Thailand population by age and province

Sun 15th October 2023

In September 2020 I downloaded and plotted some 2019 population data from the website of the National Statistical Office of Thailand. Here is the link where I downloaded the data, and this is the data I downloaded. Clicking on the link now gives population data for 2021 and 2022 in a similar format. It is not in identical format, so the R code I wrote back in 2020 will need some modifications to work on the newer data.

Back in 2020 I was shocked by the declining birth rate visible in the taper at the bottom of the pyramid.

I had a look at age distributions by province...

...and plotted population pyramids for some of the outlier provinces.
